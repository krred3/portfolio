About Me

I am a Mechanical Design Engineer (MSME) from USC with a passion for developing precision hardware and high-performance structures. My approach is grounded in bridging the gap between digital design and physical reality.

My background spans structural optimization and advanced manufacturing. At Jio, I focused on the macro scale, utilizing FEA to reduce weight in complex mechanical assemblies while maintaining structural integrity. Currently, at USC’s Zhao Group, I work on engineering fabrication processes for composite actuators with <50µm precision.

Across these projects, my goal remains the same, using tools like SolidWorks and Abaqus to predict mechanical behavior and validate designs, ensuring the final product works exactly as intended.
